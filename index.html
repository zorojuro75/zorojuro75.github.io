About me

Apart from my academic pursuits, I am also involved in extracurricular activities at IUB. As the Head of Events for the IUB E-business club, I have gained experience in event management and coordination. Additionally, I work as a researcher for JUKTI - The club of CSE, IUB, where I get to explore and learn about new technologies.

I have also had the opportunity to give back to the academic community as a Teacher Assistant, helping other students in their coursework and project work.

I believe that my diverse experiences have helped me to become a well-rounded individual with a passion for learning and personal growth. I am always eager to take on new challenges and push myself to be the best that I can be.
